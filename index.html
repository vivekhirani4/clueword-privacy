Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Clue Word app for mobile devices created by Vivek Hirani as an Ad Supported service.

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information including: IP addresses, app interactions, and device IDs. It utilizes trusted third-party services that have their own Privacy Policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics

Account and Data Deletion

Google Play requires that we provide a clear way for you to delete your account and associated data. You can perform this action at any time:

  • In-App Deletion: Open the game, go to the Settings menu, and scroll down to the "Danger Zone". Tap "Delete Account & Data". This will permanently remove your progress, coins, and profile from our cloud servers and your device.
  • Manual Request: Alternatively, you may request data deletion by emailing us at vhworld.games@gmail.com. We will process your request within a reasonable timeframe.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will immediately delete this from our servers.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, please contact us at: vhworld.games@gmail.com.
